Enterprise AI — 99.4% Accurate OCR

Our multi-model OCR stack combines layout-aware transformers with domain-specific fine-tuning. It handles handwritten text, degraded scans, rotated pages, multi-column layouts, and mixed-language documents — outputting structured JSON your databases can ingest immediately, with a per-field confidence score.

  • Handwriting, stamps & mixed-media support
  • Per-field confidence scoring
  • Domain fine-tuning (medical, legal, finance)

Invisible Cloud Pipelines

Every extraction feeds into a serverless orchestration layer that routes clean data wherever you need it — your database, your CRM, your BI dashboard, or any REST API. No manual transfers. No human bottlenecks. Documents arrive in one end; structured records appear in the other, in under 8 seconds.

  • Webhook + REST API output
  • Salesforce, HubSpot, Airtable connectors
  • Sub-8 second end-to-end latency
